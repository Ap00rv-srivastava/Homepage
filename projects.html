Research Projects

Mechanical Metamaterials, Non-Affine Deformations and Deployable Structures (August 2018 — presents)
Guide: Prof. Mandar M. Inamdar, IIT Bombay

In the first half of the project, I looked at several intriguing ideas and concepts starting with mechanisms, origami, topological insulators, form-finding, metamaterials, lattice mechanics, lattice dynamics, topological mechanics, and finally concluded with the study of mechanisms and states of self stress present inlattices, connecting all these ideas. The detailed report of the first phase of the project can be found here.

I’m currently working towards developing a program code, which will help us in studying the effect of different kinds of stimuli and tweakings in simple spring-ball models of materials.This will help us identify several ways in which connectivity can be altered, or pre-tensions can be applied to induce curvatures and different shapes and study other exciting properties such as vibrational modes and wave propagation.
